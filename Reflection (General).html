These are my general compliments to the series


The Playlist is a very nice series, it portrays
the story of the creation of Spotify really well.
Every episode shows the point of view and story
of every person that made a big contribution
to the creation of the company/app, which makes
it a lot more interesting to watch. Every episode hints
at a certain character, causing me to want to watch
the next episode even more.
