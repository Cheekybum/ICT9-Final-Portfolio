Cinematography

These are my compliments to the cinematography of the series


The cinematography is very good and the series utilized
different transitions and camera movements. They also added
CGI elements in some shots that the characters interacted with,
which was really cool to see. In every episode, the color pallet
changes according to the personality of the character being
focused on, which is very nice as it makes the
environment more fitting to the character.
